## Data Storage and Cloud Sync

JotCast offers optional cloud synchronization using Google Drive. If you choose to sign in with your Google Account:
*   **Google Drive Access:** The app requests access to its own dedicated folder on your Google Drive. This folder is hidden from your main Drive view and is used strictly to store your encrypted note backups.
*   **Authentication:** We use Firebase Authentication to securely handle your login. We do not store your Google password.

## Voice Transcription

The app uses your device's built-in speech recognition capabilities to provide voice-to-text functionality. Audio data is typically processed locally on the device or by system-level Google services; JotCast does not transmit or store your raw audio files on its own servers.
