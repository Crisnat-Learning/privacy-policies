## Privacy Policy for Realtime Speedometer

**Last updated: January 11, 2026**

**Crisnat Foundations** ("we," "our," or "us") operates the **Realtime Speedometer** mobile application (the "App"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our App and the choices you have associated with that data.

By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

### 1. Information Collection and Use

For a better experience while using our Service, we may require you to provide us with certain personally identifiable information. The information that we request will be retained on your device and is not collected by us in any way, unless explicitly stated below.

#### A. Location Data (GPS)

Our App's primary function is to display your current speed. To do this, we require access to your device's **Location Services (GPS)**.

* **How we use it:** The App reads your location coordinates in real-time to calculate your movement speed and display it on the speedometer (in km/h or mph).
* **Data Retention:** This location data is processed locally on your device in real-time. We **do not** transmit your location history to any external servers, nor do we store your travel history.

#### B. Advertising Data

To support the development and maintenance of this App, we may display third-party advertisements (banners) at the bottom of the screen. We use **Google AdMob** to serve these ads.

* AdMob and its partners may use device identifiers (such as the Android Advertising ID), cookies, and general location data (coarse location) to provide personalized or non-personalized advertising.
