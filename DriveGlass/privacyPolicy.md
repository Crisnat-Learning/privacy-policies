## Information Collection and Use

DriveGlass is designed to operate primarily offline and with maximum privacy in mind. We do not require you to create an account, and we do not store your personal data on any external servers.

However, to provide our core functionality, the app requests certain permissions and accesses specific types of data *locally* on your device:

*   **Location Data:** The app requires access to your device's precise location (GPS) to calculate speed, heading, and altitude for the "Offline HUD" feature. **This data is processed strictly locally on your device in real-time on your screen. It is never logged, stored, transmitted, or shared with us or any third-party servers.**
*   **Screen Recording / Media Projection:** Our core "HUD Mirroring" feature requires screen capture permissions to duplicate your navigation app onto a mirrored overlay. **This screen data is only streamed temporarily to your own display layer. The app does NOT record, save, transmit, or monitor your screen contents.**
*   **System Overlays:** We require permission to draw over other apps to project the HUD reflection correctly.
